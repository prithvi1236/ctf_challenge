 Challenge Title: The Whispering Cipher
🧩 Story/Intro

An anonymous whistleblower left a message containing sensitive information hidden in layers of encryption. You intercepted this note:
TmlubHZ2Zl9LZ2Z0a3FvZ2h0eWFfUWhfSnNmbg==

🎯 Goal
Recover the original plaintext message.

Hint 1:
You can decode the string using base64.

Hint 2:
Consider the Vigenère Cipher — very popular in the 19th century and uses a repeating key.

Hint 3:
Try common English words like “key”, “flag”, or even “cipher” as the key to decrypt.
